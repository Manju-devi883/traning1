## Learning Outcome
The first day focused on understanding the complete AI training roadmap. An overview of the core AI concepts, modern AI engineering roles, development approaches, and industry tools was provided. This session established the foundation for the upcoming hands-on learning throughout the one-month training program.

## Summary
Day 1 served as an orientation session, introducing the key technologies, AI tools, and concepts that will be explored in depth during the training.
